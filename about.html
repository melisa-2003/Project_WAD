Our Story

Welcome to DAYANGSARI ENT, where traditional Malaysian kuih and delicacies come to life through generations of family recipes and heartfelt passion.

We believe the best kuih comes from the heart. Each piece is handcrafted with love, using only the finest ingredients and authentic methods passed down through our family for decades.

From festive celebrations to everyday treats, our kuih brings the warmth of home and the taste of tradition to your table. Every order is prepared fresh, ensuring you receive the same quality and care that has made our family recipes beloved for generations.
